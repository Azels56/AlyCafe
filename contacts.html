About Us

Welcome to Aly's Haven

Misola and Aly shared a dream—to open a café where people could gather, unwind, and enjoy quality beverages and pastries. With dedication, hard work, and careful saving, they turned that vision into reality. Their café, Aly's Haven, is not just a business but a reflection of their journey together—a place where their passion for great food, warm hospitality, and meaningful moments comes to life. Named after Jules Alyssandria, Aly's Haven symbolizes the comfort, love, and dedication that built it, inviting every guest to be part of their story with each cup served.
