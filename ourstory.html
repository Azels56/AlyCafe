Our story

From a Dream to a Haven – What started as a simple vision between Misola and Aly became a heartfelt reality. Through passion, perseverance, and a love for great coffee and pastries, Aly's Haven was born—a place where every sip and every bite tell a story of warmth, dedication, and togetherness.

Aly's Haven is more than just a café; it's a reflection of a journey built on hard work, shared dreams, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Inspired by the idea of creating a welcoming space where people can gather, unwind, and make lasting memories, Emmanuel and Jules poured their hearts into every detail. From carefully selecting the finest ingredients to crafting a menu that balances comfort and creativity, their goal has always been to serve more than just food and drinks—they want to offer an experience. Each cup of coffee brewed and every pastry served carries a piece of their story. The name itself, Aly's Haven, is a tribute to Jules Alyssandria, symbolizing the warmth, love, and dedication that turned this dream into reality. Whether you're stopping by for a quick coffee, catching up with friends, or seeking a quiet moment of reflection, Aly's Haven welcomes you with open arms, inviting you to be part of their journey—one sip at a time. community, sensory experience, and future vision.
